    Featured Services & Announcements

    CSN Placement Services

    CSN provides free placement testing for English and Math using Guided Self-Placement and ALEKS PPL. Testing is available at all three main campuses.

    Professor/Instructor Exam Appointments

    Students taking CSN professor exams or online class exams must schedule an appointment. Walk-ins are available on a limited basis; mornings are recommended.

    Accuplacer for Other Institutions

    CSN offers proctored Accuplacer testing for other institutions for a fee. Students can provide remote vouchers or have institutions send login information.

    Testing Center Overview

    The CSN Testing Center serves as a gateway to student success at the College of Southern Nevada. We offer evidence-based placement, academic and professional exam proctoring, and remote testing services that support your academic journey from entry to graduation.

    Our staff are committed to maintaining the highest standards of test security and professional integrity, following the NCTA Professional Standards and Guidelines. CSN Testing Centers are NCTA-certified and located at all three main campuses.
